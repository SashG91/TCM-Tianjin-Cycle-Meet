Starbucks Riverview Place

Saturdays at 08:00am

Distance 30-60kms

A relatively flat course along the Haihe River. Longer sections to get your cadence going and a good opportunity to follow the course of the river and see some of the amazing European architecture. Enjoy a cup of coffee at a local cafe!


MixC Mall

Sundays at 08:00am

Distance: 50-80kms (including 6, 8 & 10% climbs)

A testing course that offers a good opportunity to get some mileage into your legs with challenging climb segments, experience some amazing views along the route. Refuel with some local food at the rest points.


IKEA Entrance A

Sundays at 09:00am

Distance: 80-100kms

This longer route begins further out of the city centre. It is a combination of flat and hill climbs. A good opportunity to see a bit of the countryside
