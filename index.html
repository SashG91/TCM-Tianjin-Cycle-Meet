About Us

Tianjin Cycle Meet (TCM) was created in the Hedong District in 2022 with the vision to get social road cyclists
riding together, with the benefit of adding some structure to their weekly training.

Why join us?

At TCM you have the flexibility of choosing the type of cycle that best suits your training needs.
From flat routes to challenge your cadence along the Haihe river to hill climbs out of the city or maybe a combination of both TCM offers an opportunity to share your passion for cycling with similar enthusiasts.

So if you are looking for some maintenance group rides or training to be the next Peter Sagan, TCM offers suitable options for you!
